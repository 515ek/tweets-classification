The aim of the application is to classify tweets as negative or postive sentiment.
The landing page provides the option to search for the tweets including specific topic or to pick any one of the either globally trending or trending in india topics.
Application retrieves the 20 popular tweets of the selected topic and classify them as positive or negative sentiments, With the probability score for the prediction. 
The WordCloud image displays the frequency of the words used in the collection of tweets being classified.
The Evaluation of the Machine Learning Model that is Used to classify tweets Can be accesed using Model Evaluation tab.
